Thoughts about Life

(Smiles).

It would be difficult to say what I think about because I think about different things at different times. But nevertheless, here are some things:

Is life a journey or a war?

Why people are the way they are?

If death is certain, can I go loco?

Why death exists and so many of us fear it?

When I wil be old what will matter the most?

Is it true that our interpretation of reality influences the way we behave, therefore our lifestyle and results?

What truly matters?

Who would visit me at my funeral?

Is love truly one of the few things that matter most?

What would be utopian society like? Do we have it now?

Are humans inherently good or bad? Maybe neither?

Why humans have such brains and other life forms do not have?

Why do we search for meaning and animals do not? Do they?

What each one of us should experience while being alive?

Is being self-centered bad? What is bad by the way?

We are not taught how to live well and how to beat the fear of the unknown. Why is that?

Why do we talk too little about the things that matter? Why do we talk too little about old age, sickness and death?

Actually, I made it a habit to replay conversations, to worry about things in life, to worry about 'what ifs' of life, and to do this every time.

It is just a habit I need to break. I need to become aware of how much mind's space and energy I needlessly spending on thinking this way. I must let things just how it should be.
